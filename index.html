Meet Liang Tze

On the first day of school, some of his friends thought he would be that quiet boy sitting in the corner of the classroom. However, all that was about to change: he was an extroverted student studying at the School of Science and Technology. Moving forward in his first year, he founded Kids Learn Code in 2022 with a group of close friends, with the mission to help underprivileged children learn to code for free.

He tries to volunteer at libraries while planning and participating in teaching sessions at Kids Learn Code while juggling his schoolwork. This is his way of taking a break and relaxing, as all these bring a sense of joy to him. In addition, he has a strong interest in and enthusiasm for transportation, coding and even photography, which he does in his other free time.

In the future, he wants to be able to pursue his passion for engineering in civil engineering, helping to improve the transportation system in Singapore. He may also consider becoming a teacher to empower and change lives. He firmly believes that a person's life is highly dependent on the teacher, as they are the ones that shape their habits.
